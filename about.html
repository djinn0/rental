About Us

We are a family with young kids who love to travel. Before having kids I was a bit of a gear head. I loved researching, buying and testing gear for my various outdoor hobbies. I build multiple bikes from components, learned how to rock climb I was always counting ounces and optimizing my ultralight backpacking setup like I was about to do a multi-month through hike, even though most of my adventures were causual just weekend jaunts. Since having kids my hobbies have changed; I don't invest as much money in gear and outdoor adventures are more kid friendly like hiking, car camping and just playing around at the park. Two things that haven't changed are I am still a gear head, and we still love to travel. After struggling to fit our oversized stroller in a rental car and dragging a heavy carseat through the airport on our first trip I decided that there had to be better equipment for traveling with children. I started researching options and quickly fell down the rabbit hole of mommy blogs and reddit posts and youtube reviews. I emerged from the deluge of information with one thought I wish I could just rent this gear for our next trip. After failing to find a rental shop near Salt Lake that specialised in travel gear for people with kids I decided to start on myself so here we are.

We live close to the Salt Lake International Airport. We purchase equipment after extensive research and test it ourselves before we rent it out to ensure that it functions to our high standards. We provide gear to Utah based families who need colapsable strollers, foldable car seats and cribs that fit in backpacks to streamline their travel experience. We also provide gear like child carriers to families traveling to Utah to experience our beautiful national parks and other outdoor adventure opportunities. In addition to childrens travel gear we are experimenting with renting niche equipment like a travel beach kit that comes with inflatable boogie boards, a sunshade, a cooler and beach toys all conviently packaged in a suitcase.
